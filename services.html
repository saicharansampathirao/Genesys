    Transition Approach:

    1. Planning Phase

    • Kick-Off Call
    • Understand the scope of the project
    • Adequate training before the inception
    • Onboarding the certified coders
    • SOP’s
    • Planning the targets and the audit plan
    • Plan for the backlog (if any)

    2. Production Phase

    • Ramp-plan of 4 weeks
    • Daily/Monthly production reports
    • Projecting the prediction of the monthly deliverables
    • Increase of the targets for every 3 months based on the time and motion study

    3. Implementation Phase

    • Reviewing the SOP’s received and provide recommendations if any and get the sign-off for the SOP and provide training
    • Weekly touch base calls to bridge the communication gaps
    • Create transparency

    4. Audit Phase

    • We follow 5 weeks audit plan which is explained in detail in the next slide
    • We also have PIP in place for the underperformers
    • Daily feedback sessions based on the error feedback

    Week 1

    QA team will perform 100% QA to ensure the coder is following the process & coding guidelines/Client specifications.

    Week 2

    QA team will confirm the coder quality consistency and efficiency, educate the coder on knowledge gaps, and reduce the QA percentage to 75%.

    Week 3

    QA team will work on coders' quality improvement and simultaneously address the coders' knowledge gaps and calibration.

    Week 4

    QA team will continue to monitor the coders' quality consistency, efficiency, and reduce QA percentage to 25%.

    Week 5

    QA team will continuously monitor the coders' quality, consistency, and efficiency, and consistently perform 5% QA.

    If at any point in this process, the coders' quality consistency falls below the expected threshold, the level of QA% reverts to the higher level of percentage.

    Continuous Learning Process:
  • We monitor the coders accuracy on daily/weekly/monthly basis and take necessary action plan to improve the quality and accuracy based on key error parameters.
  • We educate the coders in the first hour before start of production, will provide their errors feedback and training on any other new scenarios.
  • We also conduct daily huddle for 30 minutes on key areas including
    • 1. Industry coding updates & Client updates
    2. New cases scenarios
    3. Refresher training
    4. Error feed back session
    5. Verbal question on coding concepts
    6. Also, We will conduct processknowledge test on Daily/Weekly/Monthlybasis to ensure the coder follows the guidelines perfectly.
